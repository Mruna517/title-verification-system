Why Use Our Verification System?

Our platform intelligently cross-verifies your submitted title against a database of over 160,000+ registered publications maintained by the Press Registrar General of India (PRGI).

By analyzing phonetic patterns, spelling variations, disallowed words, and periodicity, we ensure your title is unique, compliant, and distinct from any existing publication.

Avoid delays, rejections, and legal conflicts. Get real-time feedback and a smart verification probability score — all in under a second.
